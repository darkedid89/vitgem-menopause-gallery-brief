Designer Workflow (Scene-Without-Bottle Method)

To avoid AI-distorted text, melting caps, or warped jars, follow this composition pipeline:

  • Get the Background Scene: The user generates the background image using ChatGPT / Midjourney prompts (without the jar in the prompt).
  • Place the Master Jar: Open the scene in Photoshop or Photopea. Drop master_bottle_cut.png into the scene at the specified position.
  • Add Lighting & Shadows: Draw a contact shadow beneath the bottle base (black, 50% opacity, multiply) and a soft floor reflection (30% opacity, Gaussian blur 20px). Blend lighting.
  • Add Text Overlay: Add text according to the exact layout specs (font sizes, leading, and casing). All text must be live/vector typography, NOT AI-rendered.
  • Export and Verify: Save as 2048×2048 sRGB JPEG at quality 92 (keep file size under 1 MB). Label as 1.jpg to 7.jpg.

How to Use Prompts in ChatGPT

When generating backgrounds, copy the prompts from this brief verbatim. For best results, follow this method:

  1. Upload the master render 1.jpg to ChatGPT to establish the bottle visual style.
  2. Paste the Background Prompt and Composition Prompt together.
  3. If the AI renders a jar or text despite the prompts, add: "Remember: Do NOT render any jar, bottle, container, or text in the scene. The center must be blank."
  4. Select the best render, download it, and hand it to the designer.

Compliance & QA Checklist

Before exporting the final JPEGs, check every image against the compliance rules:

  • Translucent Purple Gummies: Gummies must be grape-purple (#631C6C) and dome-shaped. Never orange, red, or light pink.
  • No UK Flags: Do not add flags or emblem maps. Trust is conveyed via the badges.
  • UK Manufacture Claim: Do not write "Manufactured in the UK". Use the verbatim string: MADE IN THE UK.
  • No Medical Claims: Do not use medical terms like "HRT", "relief", "cure", "treat", or "menopause symptoms". Stick strictly to authorized claims (Slot 4).
  • No Text Spelled Wrong: Double check that KSM-66® and Affron® saffron include the registered trademark symbols and are spelled correctly.
